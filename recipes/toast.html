mmm cinnamon toast

Description

Do you hunger for the toast? yes you do I know you do.

This recipie comes from my great great great great great great great great great great great great great great great great great great grandmother.

Ingridents

  • toaster
  • sliced white bread
  • sugar
  • cinnamon
  • butter

Steps

  1. Mix together cinnamon and sugar
  2. put bread in toaster for desired time
  3. spread butter on toast once it's done cooking
  4. sprinkle cinnamon sugar onto the toast
  5. enjoy
